01 — Overview

Brand identity

HaulBrokr is the digital marketplace connecting contractors with licensed dump truck operators. The brand should feel industrial, trustworthy, and premium — dark surfaces, warm metal accents, and clear hierarchy.

Personality

  • Industrial luxury — heavy industry, refined finish
  • Reliable and operational — built for the job site
  • Confident, not flashy
  • Texas-rooted, national ambition

Voice

  • Direct and practical
  • Short sentences over marketing fluff
  • Technical when needed, always clear
  • Product name is always HaulBrokr

02 — Logo

Logo system

Primary mark · Dark

App icon

Usage rules

  • Prefer the full wordmark on dark backgrounds (#141414 or darker).
  • Keep clear space around the logo equal to at least the height of the “H”.
  • Do not stretch, rotate, recolor, or add outlines/shadows to the logo.
  • Minimum digital width for the wordmark: 120px.
  • Use the app icon for mobile home screens, favicons, and avatars only.

06 — Application

Do and don’t

Do

Use dark backgrounds with gold or bronze accents for marketing and deck surfaces.

Don’t

Don’t place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a dark overlay.

Do

Keep one clear hierarchy: brand → headline → one supporting sentence → CTA.

Don’t

Don’t stretch, recolor, or outline the wordmark. Never replace “HaulBrokr” with “DumpBroker.”

Do

Use product blue (#3B82F6) for interactive UI and brand gold (#E9A600) for brand emphasis.

Don’t

Don’t mix purple glow themes or light cream “AI default” palettes into HaulBrokr surfaces.
